Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

1. Overview

VGC Calc Formatter ("the Add-on") is a Google Docs editor add-on that helps users format and organize Pokemon VGC calculation text directly inside the active document. This policy explains how the Add-on handles user information.

2. App Purpose

The Add-on is designed to speed up and standardize formatting workflows in Google Docs. It provides a sidebar UI to insert section headers and format selected calculation blocks with consistent visual styles.

3. Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The Add-on:

  • Reads and modifies only the currently open Google Document, and only when you explicitly trigger a formatting action.
  • Stores your formatting preferences (color thresholds and palette choices) in Google's own PropertiesService, scoped to your user account within the script. This data never leaves Google's infrastructure and is never accessible to the developer.
  • Loads the sidebar branding images from public Google Sites URLs so the logo can switch between light and dark themes.
  • Does not read, index, or transmit the content of your documents to any third party.

4. Google API Scopes Used

The Add-on requests only the minimum permissions required:

  • documents.currentonly – Read and write access limited to the currently open document.
  • script.storage – Store user preferences (colors and thresholds) locally within Google's infrastructure.
  • script.locale – Use the Google Workspace locale to adapt localized behavior.
  • script.container.ui – Render the add-on sidebar and UI actions inside Google Docs.

These scopes comply with the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Third-Party Services

The Add-on does not integrate with or share data with any third-party service.

6. Children's Privacy

The Add-on does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
